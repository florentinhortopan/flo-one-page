Agent of Presence

Intelligent fashion accessories where technology becomes invisible

Where Prada Meets Apple • Financed by Ballantine's

Agent of Presence explores the intersection of luxury fashion and wearable technology, creating accessories that seamlessly integrate flexible electronics and smart materials into elegant, everyday designs.

Sponsored by Ballantine's

This project was made possible through Ballantine's innovative brand initiative to finance creative expression and breakthrough design. As part of their commitment to supporting visionary projects that challenge conventions, Ballantine's sponsored the development of Agent of Presence, enabling the exploration of how luxury fashion and wearable technology can merge seamlessly.

The sponsorship represented Ballantine's dedication to fostering innovation in design and expression, supporting projects that push boundaries and create new paradigms in the intersection of craft, technology, and style. This partnership exemplifies how brands can champion experimental work that advances both aesthetic and technical frontiers.

The Vision

Concept film: Where luxury fashion aesthetics meet cutting-edge technology

Flexible Light Fabrics

The core innovation lies in flexible electronic textiles that respond to touch, movement, and environment while maintaining the drape and feel of traditional luxury materials.

Material innovation: Flexible light-emitting fabrics integrated into fashion accessories

Technical Innovation

  • Flexible OLED integration in fabric substrates
  • Touch-sensitive conductive threads
  • Ultra-thin battery systems
  • Washable electronic components
  • Sustainable materials and manufacturing

Manufacturing & Assembly

Bringing together traditional craftsmanship with modern electronics manufacturing, each piece is carefully assembled to ensure durability while maintaining aesthetic integrity.

Production process: Mounting electronic enclosures with artisanal precision

Design Development

From initial concept sketches to final product, the design process balanced aesthetic refinement with technical feasibility, material constraints, and user experience considerations.

Design Philosophy

Technology should enhance, not overshadow. Agent of Presence demonstrates that wearable electronics can be beautiful, functional, and indistinguishable from traditional luxury accessories. The best technology is the technology you forget is there.

Key Principles:

  • Technology as enhancement, not decoration
  • Seamless integration of digital and physical
  • Sustainable and ethical production
  • Timeless design over trendy tech
  • Artisanal quality meets industrial precision
