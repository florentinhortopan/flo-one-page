Industrial Design & Interaction Design

Featured Projects: From Boats to Wearables

Design Philosophy: Blending precision NURBS modeling and design-for-manufacture with human-centered innovation. These projects span naval design, medical devices, transportation, wearables, and smart textiles—demonstrating versatility across physical and digital product design.

40ft Speed Boat

Naval Design Sole Engineering • Viareggio, Italy • 2005

Challenge: Model multiple hull variants for a high-performance speed boat with precision curvature control required for Italian boat-building standards.

Solution & Impact:

  • Modeled 4 hull variants plus upper deck using advanced NURBS techniques
  • Achieved precise curvature control and fairing necessary for production manufacturing
  • Delivered complete technical sheets and sections with minimal approximation
  • Enabled one of the first Rhinoceros-designed hulls in Italy's traditional boat-building industry

Technical Stack: Rhinoceros (NURBS modeling), precision CAD for production, technical documentation
