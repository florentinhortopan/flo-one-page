The Problem

Traditional set-top box experiences were plagued with usability issues that frustrated millions of TV viewers daily:

Key Pain Points

  • Remote controls with 40+ buttons overwhelming users
  • Slow, cumbersome navigation through TV guides and menus
  • Voice control feeling tacked-on, not integrated into the experience
  • Poor content discovery leading to "I have 500 channels and nothing to watch"
  • Inconsistent interfaces across different TV providers
  • No intelligent system to learn user preferences and habits

Reimagining the Remote Control

I worked closely with hardware engineers and developers to design innovative remote control concepts that simplified interactions while adding powerful new capabilities.

A tactile, ergonomic remote that users can operate without looking, using unique button shapes and textures.

Boomerang Touchpad Hybrid

Voice UX & AI Behavior Design

I helped shape voice UX by integrating Nuance (Microsoft) natural language recognition tools, redefining user flows, and designing AI behavior based on intelligent combinations of remote buttons and voice commands.

Voice + Remote Hybrid Intelligence
