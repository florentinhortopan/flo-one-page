Puxa.ai

Chrome extension that enhances your video browsing experience with real-time AI insights, transforming passive viewing into actionable learning and shopping experiences.

Role: Co-founder & Chief Design Officer

Duration: 2023-Present

Website: puxa.ai

Key Features:

  • Instant Video Summaries - Get AI-powered summaries of video content in seconds
  • Key Takeaways - Extract the most important points from any video
  • Personalized Recommendations - Discover relevant content based on your interests
  • Product Identification - Identify products mentioned in videos with direct shopping links
  • Cross-Platform Support - Works seamlessly across various video platforms

How It Works:

  • Install the Chrome extension from the Puxa.ai website
  • Browse videos on your favorite platforms as usual
  • Get instant AI insights, summaries, and recommendations
  • Click on identified products to shop directly

Design Philosophy:

  • Seamless integration with existing video browsing habits
  • Non-intrusive AI assistance that enhances rather than disrupts
  • Focus on actionable insights and practical value
  • Clean, minimal interface that respects user attention

Impact:

  • Transforms passive video watching into active learning
  • Saves time with instant summaries and key takeaways
  • Bridges the gap between content discovery and action
  • Enhances shopping experience with smart product identification
