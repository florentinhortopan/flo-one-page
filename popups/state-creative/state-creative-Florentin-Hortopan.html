DCAC - DC Abortion Fund

Website redesign for the DC Abortion Fund, a nonprofit organization providing financial assistance and support. The project focused on creating an accessible, empathetic digital experience that respects user privacy while clearly communicating services and resources.

Key Features: Privacy-first design, accessible navigation, clear call-to-actions, donation integration, resource library, and empathetic messaging that respects sensitive subject matter.

EAOP - Educational Assistance

Digital presence for an educational assistance program, designed to help students and families navigate support resources. Clean, information-focused design with emphasis on usability and accessibility.

Approach: Student-first design language, intuitive information architecture, mobile-responsive layouts, and clear pathways to support resources.
