Talks & Presentations

Speaking on Wearable Tech, Innovation & Design

Selected speaking engagements covering wearable technology, smart textiles, fashion tech, social innovation, and design thinking. From international conferences to local innovation meetups.

2014

Wearable Tech & Smart Fabrics

PCH Highway1 / Lime Lab • San Francisco

Presented Switch Embassy's tshirtOS™ wearable technology at PCH's premier hardware accelerator. Covered technical challenges of creating washable, flexible LED displays integrated into textiles, and the journey from concept to manufacture. Discussed design-for-manufacture considerations for smart textiles and the future of wearable technology.

2012

Fashion Tech, IoT & Smart Fabrics

LeWeb Paris • Paris, France

Showcased Agent of Presence at Europe's leading tech conference, demonstrating the intersection of luxury fashion and wearable electronics. Presented vision for "technology becoming invisible" through elegant integration of light-emitting fabrics into high-end accessories. Discussed challenges of combining traditional craftsmanship with flexible electronics.

2010

Social Open Governance & New Media for EU Projects

EU Thematic Dissemination • Florence, Italy

Explored the role of new media and open governance models in European Union funded projects. Discussed how digital tools can enhance transparency, participation, and outcomes in publicly funded initiatives. Shared case studies from RICUD network implementation.

2010

Cofarming & the End of Food Purchase

ToscanaLab / Ignite Italia • Tuscany

Presented innovative models for collaborative food production and distribution. Explored how technology and community organization could transform food systems from purchase-based to participation-based models. Part of the Ignite fast-paced presentation format (20 slides, 15 seconds each).

2009

Notes on Participated Design / Crowdfunding 2010

Pecha Kucha Night

Explored participatory design methodologies and the emerging crowdfunding landscape. Presented in the Pecha Kucha format (20 images × 20 seconds), discussing how designers can involve stakeholders throughout the creative process and how crowdfunding was beginning to democratize product development.

Speaking Interests: Wearable technology, smart textiles, fashion tech, design for manufacture, participatory design, social innovation, crowdfunding, and the intersection of traditional crafts with emerging technology.
